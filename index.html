Welcome to civic centric reporting system

This website lets citizens of Tamil Nadu report civic issues like potholes, broken streetlights,
water leaks, garbage problems, and more. You can submit issues in Tamil or English,
upload photos, mark locations, and track whether problems are resolved. Your
report is visible to everyone, ensuring transparency and preventing false closures.
Old or unresolved issues are automatically prioritized so they get noticed.
Together, we can make our cities safer, cleaner, and better for everyone
