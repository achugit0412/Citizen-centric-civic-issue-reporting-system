What is MyCityFix?

MyCityFix is a platform to report and track civic issues in your city...


How to Submit a Report

  1. Enter your name and district.
  2. Describe the issue clearly.
  3. Upload an image (optional).
  4. Click the Report button to submit.

How to View Your Reports

Go to the MyReport page to track your reports.
